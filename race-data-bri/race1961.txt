														<h1>1961 Paris - Roubaix </h1>
														<h2>59th edition: Sunday, April 9, 1961</h2>
													</div>
												<!-- InstanceEndEditable -->
  <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="PageText" -->
	<p><a href="paris-roubaix-index.html">Paris-Roubaix podium history</a> | <a href="pr1962.html">1962 edition</a> | <a href="pr1960.html">1960 edition</a></p>
												<p>263.5 km. 121 classified finishers. </p>
												<p><img src="../../images-all/classics-images/paris-roubaix-hist/1961-PR-62-65-Van-Looy-Rik.jpg" width="414" height="590" alt="Rik van Looy" /></p>
												<p class="cap">Rik van Looy</p>
	<p>As a result of an earlier attack by Henry Anglade, six riders (five of them Belgians) entered the Roubaix stadium. Emile Daems led out the sprint, but van Looy was enjoying fabulous form and easily won. He did have to be cautious, however, because his rear tire had a slow leak and he was afraid of rolling it on the banking.</p>
