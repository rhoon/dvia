														<h1>1953 Paris - Roubaix </h1>
														<h2>51st edition: Sunday, April 12, 1953</h2>
													</div>
												<!-- InstanceEndEditable -->
  <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="PageText" -->
	<p><a href="paris-roubaix-index.html">Paris-Roubaix podium history</a> | <a href="pr1954.html">1954 edition</a> | <a href="pr1952.html">1952 edition</a></p>
												<p>245 km. Unknown number of starters and classified finishers, records only go 75 deep.	</p>
												<p>Twenty kilometers from the finish five riders were away: Donato Piazza, Wout Wagtmans, Germain Derycke, Raphaël Géminiani and Adolfo Grosso. Grosso and Géminiani dropped off with bike problems. Piazza escaped the remaining trio and Derycke bridged, the two coming into the Roubaix velodrome together. In the velodrome Piazza attacked first, but Derycke sped by on the finishing straight.</p>
