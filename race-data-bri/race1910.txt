														<h1>1910 Paris - Roubaix </h1>
														<h2>15th edition: Sunday, March 27, 1910</h2>
													</div>
												<!-- InstanceEndEditable -->
  <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="PageText" -->
	<p><a href="paris-roubaix-index.html">Paris-Roubaix podium history</a> | <a href="pr1909.html">1909 edition</a> | <a href="pr1911.html">1911 edition </a></p>
												<p>266 km. 145 starters and 69 (or perhaps 74) classified finishers.	Time splits after 14th place were rounded to nearest minute and are not available after 45th place.</p>
												<p>Octave Lapize and Cyrille Van Houwaert separated themselves from the field well before the race's finish in the new Roubaix velodrome. Van Houwaert was in the front for the first five laps on the track but the faster Lapize slipped by the Belgian to win his second consecutive Paris-Roubaix.</p>
