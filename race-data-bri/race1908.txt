													<p>271 km. 25.63 km/hr average speed</p>
													<p>90 riders started, 27 classified finishers													</p>
													<p>Weather the week before the race was terrible, turning the roads into a mess. Cold rain, snow and sleet on race day. Cyrille Van Houwaert arrived at the velodrome alone, several minutes ahead of his chasers, and ran into a groundsman. He remounted and finished his six laps to become the first Belgian winner of Paris-Roubaix.</p>
