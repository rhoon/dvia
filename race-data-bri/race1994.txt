                                                    <p><strong>The Race:</strong> Heavy rain for several days before the start with rain, snow, sleet and cold wind the day before the race. On race morning, strong wind, black rain clouds, snow. The temperature was 36F at the start. The snow and rain abated before the riders hit the first section of pavé. </p>
													<p>About 20 kilometers past the Wallers-Arenberg Forest the front group consisted of Andrei Tchmil, Nico Verhoeven, <a href="../../photo-galleries/rider-gallery/museeuw-johan.html">Johan Museeuw</a>, Adri Van der Poel, Franco Ballerini and Bruno Boscardin. Tchmil attacked and quickly got a gap. He soloed the last 60 km to win alone, despite a spirited chase by a group that included Gilbert Duclos-Lassalle and Johan Museeuw.</p>
