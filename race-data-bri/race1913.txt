														<h1>1913 Paris - Roubaix </h1>
														<h2>18th edition: Sunday, March 23, 1913</h2>
													</div>
												<!-- InstanceEndEditable -->
  <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="PageText" -->
	<p><a href="paris-roubaix-index.html">Paris-Roubaix podium history</a> | <a href="pr1912.html">1912 edition</a> | <a href="pr1914.html">1914 edition</a></p>
												<p>266 km. Unknown number of starters and  85 classified finishers.	Time splits to 24th place were rounded	and are unavailable for riders who finished after that.</p>
												<p>Cold. Sleet during the final kilometers. Winner François Faber had been dropped by the leading group but caught them on a downhill portion. Charles Deruyter led for most of the three laps on the Roubaix velodrome but Faber, who had suffered so much misfortune in previous editions of Paris-Roubaix, passed Deruyter just at the line.</p>
												<p><img src="../../images-all/classics-images/paris-roubaix-hist/1913-PR-faber.jpg" width="350" height="555" alt="François Faber" /></p>
