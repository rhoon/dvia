														<h2>61st edition: Sunday, April 7, 1963</h2>
													</div>

	<p><a href="paris-roubaix-index.html">Paris-Roubaix podium history</a> | <a href="pr1964.html">1964 edition</a> | <a href="pr1962.html">1962 edition</a></p>
												<p>266 km. 78 classified finishers. Nineteen riders were awarded 19th place.</p>
												<p>Noël Foré had been away for twenty kilometers when, with about two kilometers to go he was caught by Rolf Wolfshohl. Shortly thereafter Rik van Looy dragged a first-class chase group to Wolfshohl, meaning it would be a big drag race on the Roubaix velodrome. Van Looy went high up the banking and diving down through the hole sped Emile Daems, who could not be caught.</p>
