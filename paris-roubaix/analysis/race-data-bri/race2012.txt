          <h1><b>2012 Paris - Roubaix</b></h1>

          <h3><b>110th edition: Results: Sunday, April 8</b></h3>
          <h3><b>Complete results, map and photos<br />
            
          </b></h3>

        </div>

        <!-- InstanceEndEditable -->
  <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="PageText" -->

        <p> Paris (Compiègne) - Roubaix: 257.5 km with 27 sectors of cobbles totaling 51.5 km</p>

        <p>Paris-Roubaix <a href="paris-roubaix-index.html">podium history</a>

          |<a href="pr2011.html"> 2011 edition</a> | <a href="pr2013.html">2013 edition</a> | <a href="#results">Results</a> | <a href="#results">Race Day Photos</a> | <a href="#pre">Pre-Race Photos</a> | <a href="#map">Race Route</a> | <a href="#sectors">Cobbled Sectors</a> |</p>

        <p><img width="600" height="398" border="0" src="../../images-all/annual-images/images09/PR1.jpg"


            alt="" /></p>

        <hr />
        <p><strong>The Race:</strong> What a ride! With 55 km to go <a href="../../photo-galleries/rider-gallery/boonen-tom.html">Boonen</a> rode away from a helpless field. He initially took along Alessandro Ballan and Filippo Pozzato and teammmate Niki Terpstra, but 2 km later he left the Italians behind and then Terpstra couldn't stay with Boonen. Boonen then-time trialed all the way to the Roubaix velodrome. He has now equaled <a href="../../photo-galleries/rider-gallery/de-vlaeminck-roger.html">Roger de Vlaeminck's</a> record of 4 Paris-Roubaix wins and is the only rider to have done the Flanders-Roubaix double twice. </p>
