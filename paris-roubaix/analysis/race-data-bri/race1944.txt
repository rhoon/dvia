														<h2>42nd edition: Sunday, April 9, 1944</h2>
													</div>

  <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="PageText" -->
	<p><a href="paris-roubaix-index.html">Paris-Roubaix podium history</a> | <a href="pr1945.html">1945 edition</a> | <a href="pr1943.html">1943 edition </a></p>
												<p>246 km. 172 starters and 89 classified finishers	</p>
												<p>Mild and sunny weather. With 20 kilometers to go, six men were off the front. They were delayed for 20 seconds by a closed train crossing, allowing another five riders to join them. 1937 Paris-Roubaix winner Jules Rossi had teammate Lucien Vlaemynck give him a leadout, but Maurice De Sempelaere went flashing by the confident Italian. </p>
												<p>Germany still occupied France in April of 1944 and journalists returning home from the finish were bombed. 1947 Tour de France winner Jean Robic crashed badly, suffering a fractured skull and from then on wore his famous leather helmet.	</p>
