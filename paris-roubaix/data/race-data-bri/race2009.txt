Just as the leaders started the Carrefour de l'Arbre sector, Juan Antonio Flecha crashed, taking down and delaying all but Tom Boonen, who was in the lead at the time. Because of the crowd noise, at first Boonen was unaware he was alone. Filippo Pozzato chased, but Boonen was not to be caught as he won his third Paris-Roubaix.</p>
