														<h1>1937 Paris - Roubaix </h1>
														<h2>38th edition: Sunday, March 28, 1937</h2>
													</div>
												<!-- InstanceEndEditable -->
  <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="PageText" -->
	<p><a href="paris-roubaix-index.html">Paris-Roubaix podium history</a> | <a href="pr1938.html">1938 edition</a> | <a href="pr1936.html">1936 edition</a></p>
												<p>255 km. 159 starters and 50 classified finishers	</p>
												<p>Hard rain. Jules Rossi entered Roubaix with four Belgians, each individually ambitous to win and uninterested in coordinating their efforts against the Italian. With two kilometers to go Rossi led at such an extraordinary speed the others could not even pull alongside. The race finished in Roubaix on Avenue Gustave Delory (not the velodrome) where Rossi led out the sprint and won by several bike lengths.</p>
												<p>Rossi was the first Italian to win Paris-Roubaix, succeeding where Gaetoni Belloni, <a href="../../photo-galleries/rider-gallery/girardengo-costante.html">Costante Girardengo</a> and Alfredo Binda had failed.</p>
												<p><img src="../../images-all/classics-images/paris-roubaix-hist/1937-PR-Rossi-Jules.jpg" width="365" height="412" alt="Jules Rossi" /></p>
