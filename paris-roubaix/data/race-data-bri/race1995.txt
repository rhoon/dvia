														<h1>93th Paris - Roubaix (World Cup)<br />
															
															
															
										
										
										
										
										
										
										
																			
																		
																		
																	
																		
																		
																		
																	
										
										Sunday, April 9, 1995</h1>
													</div>
												<!-- InstanceEndEditable -->
  <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="PageText" -->
												<p><a href="paris-roubaix-index.html">Paris-Roubaix podium history</a> | <a href="pr1994.html">1994 edition</a> | <a href="pr1996.html">1996 edition</a> | <a href="#race">Race synopsis</a> | <a href="#results">Complete results</a></p>
												<p>266.5 km. 178 starters, 70 classified finishers.	Another	twenty	(<a href="#hd">places	71	-	91</a>)	finished	after	the		time	cutoff												</p>
												<p><img src="../../images-all/classics-images/paris-roubaix-hist/1995-PR-Ballerini-Franco.jpg" width="635" height="427" alt="Franco Ballerini wins the 1995 Paris-Roubaix" /></p>
												<p class="cap">Franco Ballerini wins</p>
												<p class="cap"><img src="../../images-all/classics-images/paris-roubaix-hist/1995-PR-Ballerini-podium.jpg" width="525" height="788" alt="Franco Ballerini wins the 1995 Paris Roubiax" /></p>
												<p class="cap">The flowers and cobblestone trophy are Ballerini's.</p>
												<p><strong><a name="race" id="race"></a>The Race: </strong>It was down to six strong men with 32 kilometers left to go. At that point Franco Ballerini took off and rode into Roubaix with almost two minutes in hand. After years of bad luck (and a very near win in 1993), Ballerini had finally won his favorite race.</p>
